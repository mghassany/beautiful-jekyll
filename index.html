Brief Bio

TEST I am currently an associate professor at Leonardo da Vinci Engineering School in Paris La Défense. I work principally on Machine Learning research subjects. I teach Machine Learning, Probability and Statistics.

Previously, I worked as a research engineer and a data scientist at Telecom Business School in Paris. I was also a post-doctoral researcher at Ecole Normale Supérieure Paris-Saclay.

I obtained my PhD (Nov. 2013) in Computer Science from Université Paris 13, where I worked in A3 team of LIPN under the supervision of Professor Younès Bennani.
I obtained my MS in Applied Mathematics & Statistics from Université Grenoble Alpes.
